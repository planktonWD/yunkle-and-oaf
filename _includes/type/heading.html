Headings

These are the available headings with their variation

Things to note: the sight uses fast fonts proxima nova, thin, light, regular and bold

Here are the variables - $font-thin:'Proxima N W01 Thin Reg'; $font-light: 'Proxima N W01 Light'; $font-regular: 'Proxima N W01 Reg'; $font-bold: 'Proxima N W01 Bold';

Wicked classes are (size)--heading makes it bold, .light-heading makes it light, .highlight gives it a highlight
